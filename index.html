Fair Play & Safety Policy

Building a respectful, school-approved learning environment

MentorLoop is committed to providing a safe, respectful, and educational environment for all students, teachers, and mentors. These Fair Play rules ensure that learning remains positive and inclusive.

1. Respectful Communication

  • Use polite and appropriate language at all times.
  • No bullying, harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • Disagreements must be academic and respectful.

2. Academic Integrity

  • Questions must be related to learning and academics.
  • No cheating, plagiarism, or sharing of exam answers.
  • Mentors should guide, not simply give answers.

3. Responsible Participation

  • Stay on-topic and avoid spam or irrelevant content.
  • Do not misuse the platform for promotion or messaging.
  • Encourage others and support a growth mindset.

4. Moderation & Consequences

  • Content may be reviewed by teachers or moderators.
  • Warnings may be issued for rule violations.
  • Repeated or serious violations can lead to suspension.

5. Student Safety

  • No sharing of personal contact information.
  • All interactions must remain within the platform.
  • Any safety concerns should be reported immediately.

By using MentorLoop, all users agree to follow these Fair Play rules and help maintain a positive learning community.
